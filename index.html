Summary
  • Core diary data is stored on your device by default.

1. Scope of the App

Today Energy Check is a wellness planning tool. It helps you record how you feel, view a simple daily score, see trends, and optionally receive gentle pacing suggestions. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency guidance.

2. Health information & self-assessment (not a diagnosis)

The number displayed in the App is a self-assessment calculated from information you enter (such as symptom levels and optional inputs). It is not a medical diagnosis, laboratory result, clinical risk score, or substitute for professional medical advice. For emergencies, contact local emergency services or your clinician.

Educational citations (third-party). The App includes a screen titled “Health information & sources” (also reachable from Settings) with links to independent educational materials for general wellness reading, including:

  • MedlinePlus (NIH) — Fatigue overview
  • MedlinePlus (NIH) — Fatigue topic page
  • WHO — Physical activity fact sheet

These third-party sites are for general education only. They do not endorse the App and are not used to compute a medical diagnosis inside the App.

Third-party AI (OpenAI)

If you enable optional AI personalization, the App sends a minimized structured summary of your saved check-ins (dates; whether you indicated dialysis the prior day; fatigue, nausea, and dizziness levels on a 0–2 scale) to the app operator’s backend server you configure (EXPO_PUBLIC_AI_SERVER_URL). That server may forward a text prompt derived from this summary to OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C.) via the OpenAI API to generate short wellness-planning suggestions. OpenAI acts as a subprocessor for this feature. OpenAI’s privacy practices are described in their policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/. The App shows an in-app disclosure (what is sent and who receives it) and asks for your confirmation before this optional transmission.

3. Information we process

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Health-related inputs you enter (e.g., symptom levels, optional weight, optional notes about your day). Stored locally on your device unless you export or use optional features below.
  • App settings (e.g., language, AI opt-in).
  • Subscription status as reflected by Apple/Google to unlock Pro features (we do not receive your full payment card number).
  • AI inputs (optional): If you enable AI personalization, a minimized summary of recent check-ins (without identifiers) may be transmitted to our server; prompts may be processed by OpenAI as described in “Third-party AI (OpenAI)” above.
  • Family share links (optional): If you create a shareable link, a limited summary may be stored on our server so the link can open in a web browser; links expire after a limited period.
  • Support communications if you email us (e.g., your email address and message content).
