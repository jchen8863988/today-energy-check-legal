Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jian Chen (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the mobile application Today Energy Check (the “App”).

Contact: jianchen8863988@gmail.com

Summary
  • Core diary data is stored on your device by default.
  • The in-app score is a self-assessment, not a medical diagnosis.
  • Optional subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) and/or Google (Google Play).
  • Optional AI is off by default; if enabled, a minimized summary may be sent to our server.
  • Optional family share links may store a limited summary on our server temporarily.

3. Information we process

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Health-related inputs you enter (e.g., symptom levels, optional weight, optional notes about your day). Stored locally on your device unless you export or use optional features below.
  • App settings (e.g., language, AI opt-in).
  • Subscription status as reflected by Apple/Google to unlock Pro features (we do not receive your full payment card number).
  • AI inputs (optional): If you enable AI personalization, a minimized summary of recent check-ins (without identifiers) may be transmitted to our server to generate suggestions.
  • Family share links (optional): If you create a shareable link, a limited summary may be stored on our server so the link can open in a web browser; links expire after a limited period.
  • Support communications if you email us (e.g., your email address and message content).

4. Subscriptions, payments & Terms of Use (EULA)

Optional Pro features may require an active auto-renewable subscription purchased through the Apple App Store and/or Google Play. Payment processing is handled by Apple/Google. We do not collect or store your full payment card number.

Apple (App Store) — Standard EULA: https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/

Subscription title, length, price, auto-renewal, and cancellation are presented at purchase time and can be managed in your Apple ID or Google account subscription settings.

5. How we use information

  • Provide the App (display scores, trends, optional Pro features).
  • Subscription entitlement verification (via Apple/Google mechanisms and, if applicable, our server verification).
  • Optional AI (only if you opt in).
  • Optional family links (only if you generate a link).
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with law and protect security where required.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed on our systems. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port personal data. For data stored only on your device, you can use Export or Delete in Settings (where available). To exercise rights related to data processed by us, contact jianchen8863988@gmail.com.

10. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

11. International users

If you use the App from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

We may update this Privacy Policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we post revisions. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
